# AGENTS.md

This repo contains a single-user LinkedIn drafting app for Jakob Berger.

## Product intent

The app should help turn source material into post angles and draft posts in Jakob's own voice.
It should feel like an editorial partner, not a template machine.

## Guardrails

- prioritize sharp, credible, peer-to-peer writing
- no generic finance thought leadership tone
- no promotional company-page language
- no em dashes in generated posts
- avoid starting with "I"
- prefer insight and operator implication over summary

## Scope

Keep the app lightweight.
Do not add auth, publishing, or team workflows unless explicitly requested.
